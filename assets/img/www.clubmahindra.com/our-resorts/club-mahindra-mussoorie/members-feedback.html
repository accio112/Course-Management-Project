Club Mahindra Mussoorie Resort Reviews

Club Mahindra Mussoorie

After a disappointing stay in Rishikesh, we travelled to this lovely hotel in the hills. Our room overlooked the mountains and valley below. Rooms were large and spacious with a writing desk and all necessary amenities. The best part is the service and attitude of staff and we felt very looked after. room service is quick and responsive and the food is the best we had during our journey. The Dal Makhni and Rice with Himalayan herbs a must try. They even arranged a excursion for us to the lovely Kempty Falls and nice short meandering drive. Would recommend this place for couples and families.

Madhuri Nene


Club Mahindra Mussoorie

Mast Hoilday @ Mussoorie

If you want a break @ 6500 ft ASL, Mussoorie is the place. A breath taking view of Dehradun from the hotel property. Very homely n courteous staff, spacious room and a large spread for all meals.
